From Director's Desk

Dear Students,

It is with immense pride and a deep sense of responsibility that I welcome you to Lady Irwin College — a pioneering institution of the University of Delhi, founded in 1932 with a singular vision: to empower women through knowledge, service, and societal leadership.

Our college was born in the crucible of India’s freedom movement — envisioned by educationists, reformers, and nation-builders who believed that the education of women was central to the making of a just and self-reliant society. We carry this legacy forward with unwavering commitment. For over nine decades.

Prof. Neelima Asthana
Director, Lady Irwin College
