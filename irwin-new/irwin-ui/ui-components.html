Overview of the Governing Body

The Governing Body is the apex administrative and executive entity of Lady Irwin College. Established under the ordinances of the University of Delhi, it holds the essential responsibility of charting the strategic vision, managing financial governance, and ensuring academic excellence within the institution.

Core Responsibilities:

  • Charting the long-term strategic vision for the college legacy.
  • Meticulously managing financial and structural governance.
  • Ensuring that academic excellence scales efficiently and seamlessly.
  • Reviewing and ratifying institutional frameworks and major developments.

For more deep historic archives, please refer to the Official Charter Documentation or consult the newly published 2024 Policy Directives directly on the site.

Food, Nutrition & Food Technology

The Department of Food and Nutrition at Lady Irwin College has been a premier hub of research and academic excellence since its inception. We emphasize holistic understanding of food science, human nutrition, and public health.

Key Objectives:

  • To impart scientific knowledge regarding food science and clinical nutrition.
  • To train students in advanced laboratory techniques and food technology.
  • To foster community-oriented research in public health nutrition.
